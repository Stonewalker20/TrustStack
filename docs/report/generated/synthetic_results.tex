We replaced the earlier experimental-plan placeholder with a reproducible synthetic benchmark executed directly on the TrustStack backend. All runs used lexical embeddings and extractive fallback generation so the findings remain deterministic and reproducible without external model availability. The benchmark covered 4 synthetic corpus conditions spanning aligned procedures, contradictory revisions, unsafe override guidance, and sparse evidence.
